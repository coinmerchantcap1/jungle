Presentation

DFT, or Designtralized Finance, is a blockchain-based platform that is disrupting the design industry by leveraging the power of decentralized technology. The platform aims to address various challenges that designers and creative professionals face, such as lack of access to design resources, difficulty in finding work, and issues with intellectual property rights.

MANAGEMENT

Managing all the design-related
resources, such as assets, templates,
tools, software, and intellectual
property, that are required for
creating designs and help businesses,

MARKETPLACE

The DSM is one of the main services
provided by the Designtralized
platform. It is a decentralized
digital marketplace that connects
businesses and individuals in need
of design services with a global
network of professional designers.

CONTEST

Feature of enables users to host design
competitions to get the best design
for their projects and provides a platform
for individuals and companies to post
a design brief and receive submissions
from designers around the world.
